Semlar, the rebellious, and radically different organization from Brazil has no handbooks or rules. But itis insanely successful. But it may not be the case everywhere.

Rulebooks have a problem. They specify rules and as a result scream to the people - We cannot trust you. But it need not be like that always. While Semlar may not have a rulebook, most humans need a guide - which will tell them more about the company.

Imagine the best workplace ever - your dream workplace. The following pages contain the employee handbook for that dream workplace - which we call as The Dream Workplace. This is a work of imagination, but you don't have to hold back. Go ahead and implement it in your organization. At least some parts of it. Drive your employee engagement to the next level and create a happy set of employees.
