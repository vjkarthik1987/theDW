Chapter 8: That is not the only good thing

We believe that an employee can work well only if we treat them well. An employee has a multitude of options to choose from and it is our prerogative to make sure that our employees are happy. To make sure our employees are happy, we follow the 5P framework that is used by Microsoft. The same has been given below. We don’t feel ashamed when we copy something. We copy something because it is good.

So let us start off with pay

As you start your life with TDW, we offer you a 10% hike from your last job and if you are a fresher, we offer you 10% more than the salary of the freshers at the same roles across a list of companies we have published in our internal website.

This is the fixed pay - you get it no matter what.

On top of this, you are eligible to receive a 25% bonus based on your performance. We have already mentioned the measurement methodology in the section about OKRs.

That is not all - on top of this, you are also eligible to receive a bonus linked to the company's performance. We believe that TDW grows because of you and hence it is our responsibility to share the profits with you. 30% of the earnings before tax and depreciation is uniformly divided amongst all the employees - irrespective of experience, seniority, or location.

You can also convert a quarter of your performance-based bonus for purchasing stock options at a discounted price. The discounted price and the available number of stock options are reviewed on a quarterly basis and are available on our internal website.

Our salary increments are rolled out twice a year based on two factors - the inflation rate over the last six months in your geographical location, and the increment based on your performance. These increments are rolled out in April and October.

So what about the perks

Our perks are offered based on what we feel can help you out. If you have any suggestions, please do tell your leader or the HR team, and we will certainly look into it.

  • You get access to a list of subscriptions - Headspace, Spotify, a gym membership (or any health subscription), Evernote or Todoist, IFTTT, Brainfm, Forest, and Medium. In case you use it, go and claim it - no questions asked.

  • Team leaders have a budget every quarter to reward performers - our ‘Star’ award. This is a coupon worth $100 that you can use for anything once you receive it.

  • Did we mention that we work only four days a week?

  • You also can choose your work schedule, as long as you are able to manage the collaboration and communication going on with your team.

  • You also get an amount for relocation every year, if you wish to change your base location.

  • Yours, your spouse’s, your parents, and your children’s medical insurance is upon us. You don’t have to pay any fee.

  • Every new child is a bundle of joy and we really want to welcome the new one with you. Any new parent (either through childbirth or through adoption) gets a stipend of $1,000. It is for the little champion.

  • We also cover any fertility-related costs for you or your partner. No questions asked.

  • You can technically work from anywhere. Read the section on how we work and you will get the answer.

  • You will get an annual stipend of up to $500 to set up your home office, and till you are with us, your broadband, phone, and Netflix subscriptions are on us.

  • You will also get a stipend that will make your home office and home greener (read more plants and trees, not paint it green).

  • Take your significant other to a magical dinner or your kids to a theme park. We will cover your dinner or ticket costs once a year.

  • You will also get $1000 to splurge on yourself during your birthdays.

  • We pay 20% of your monthly instalments (as long as you are with TDW) for any electric vehicle that your purchase (as long as it is within the limits mentioned in our internal site).

  • In case you decide to commute to the office or a co-working space using any means of public transport, it is upon us.

  • After a four-year period with us, you will get a five-week break - to do anything you wish, as long as you do not get abducted by aliens. You will also get a $2500 bonus towards your goals.

  • Every year, you can work towards a personal goal of yours for a period of 15 working days - no questions asked. It can be your own business or any other item.

  • In case you come to our office, lunch is on us. And we don’t mind you wearing any dress to the office, as long as you wear a dress.

  • We believe in rewarding you with equity. For the details, read the section on performance and OKRs.

  • You can enrol for any educational program that you wish, and on completion, you will receive the fees (with an upper limit of $5000 every two years) as a reimbursement.

  • You can buy any book from Kindle, and we will take reimburse the cost.

  • In the unlikely event of your death, we will pay 50% of your salary every month for the next seven years to your spouse or your child or your nominee.

  • We are an equal-pay employer and every year, we will make adjustments and corrections to correct the gaps if any.

  • We deposit 5% of your annual pay (not included as part of your salary component) every year in a growth fund. This is for you for your retirement or for a rainy day. But you are free to use it earlier also, but we recommend you hold back.

  • n case you are not able to work from home, you get a stipend to work from a co-working space or a coffee shop.

  • You and your team will get a stipend to spend ten days in any location of your choice (as long as it is not a war-strived country) to come together and work. Our internal coordination team will support you do the necessary arrangements so that we are within the budget that we have for each team.

  • We match your contributions (up to $5000 every year) to any recognized charity or non-profit organization.

This list is not complete, and not final. We will keep on updating this list as we learn and the world evolves.

So, what about the people?

You will work with some really amazing people from across different countries and different cultures. We really care about our people and believe that investing in them can help them grow, and also help us grow. As we told you, we put you at the centre of everything we do. And that will always be the case.
