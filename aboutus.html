About TDW

What is TDW?

TDW is an open initiative to provide insights and create awareness about the need for organizations across the world to create a better way of engaging with employees. The pandemic, along with it's hardships, forced a lot of people to work away from the established norms of working from an office. Suddenly, employees were attuned to a new way of working. And as the pandemic abated, many organizations went back to the old way of working which led to trends such "The Great Resignation" and "Quiet Quitting" or even "Quick Qutting" because organizations were trying to get back to the comforts of established practices of management.

TDW aims to provide organizations and employees alike with insights which can be implemented at various organizations across the world.

What is the TDW handbook?

The TDW Handbook is the employee handbook of a fictional company called "The Dream Workplace" aka TDW. No one except the Wakandans know what the TDW does. Some say that they are a super-secret organization who are the representatives of extra-terrestrial beings. Some others say that the TDW is a figment of some disgruntled imagination. But, they apparently have a workforce that is super-delighted. And organization leaders apparently want to know the secret recipe.
