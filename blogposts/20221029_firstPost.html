Post 1-Setting the Ground Rules

29 Oct 2022

Hello World. Just setting up the blog. This is the first post. The kind of things that you say in your first program.

Jokes apart, this post is intended to set the context for this website and define the ground rules.

While we have only four levels, we have lots of teams. And each of these teams comprises a lot of people doing a lot of things. And all of us are part of a tribe. We know it can get confusing, so let us explain.

First and foremost, this website aka the blog aka the TDW website is meant to be an open-source initiative to learn about the way organizations, read the really good ones, are engaging with their employees to make them happy. The vision is to create the largest repository of examples for employees and organizations to get inspired to create an engagement strategy for their employees. Long story short, we want to be something like Forbes for employee engagement.

This leads us to the ground rules

  • We are and will stay as a blog. Whatever else we will do will be ancillary.

  • We will not have a subscriber list.

  • We will not send newsletters or mailers promoting our articles

  • We will never have a paywall for our articles or written content.

  • We will not use SEO to attract an audience to our blog.

  • We are Ok with ads and will display ads, but we as an organization will not collect any data of yours.

That's it. The rest will follow. Please extend your support. And please buy us a coffee.
