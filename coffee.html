Contribute to this open initiative

We see TDW as an open initiative through which we can empower employees with ideas which can make their workplaces better. While we do have an idea about how we want to take TDW ahead, we are clear about few things. 1) We will not put a paywall of any kind in accessing the content in our website; 2) We will not have an email subscription list and we will not mail you on a regular basis; 3) We will not track your behaviour; and 4) We believe that this site is evolving and we welcome ideas. Please share your ideas here.

Hence, we request you one thing. If you like the website, and find it relevant, please donate to this. This will help us take this forward and keep it running. We thank you for your contribution.
