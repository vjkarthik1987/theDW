BambooHR treats birthdays as paid holidays.

Bobbie has a parental leave policy, also known as TakeOurLeave, which offers birthing and non-birthing parents up to 12 months of leave to bond with their baby. Many companies give parental leave only to mothers.

Learn more

Full Contact, a Denver based company pays its employees $7500 if they're going off the grid while on vacation. This perk is paid out only if the employees can prove that they are completely off the grid during their time off by not checking work emails, texts, or calls; and absolutely no work of any kind.

Learn more

Hubspot has a company wide week-off in July.

Learn more

Hubspot has unlimited vacation. The employees can decide when and how long to take time off. They also have a vacation quota relief to make sure that it is unlimited for all.

Learn more

Hubspot provides a four-week sabbatical, after five years with Hubspot with an equivalent of $5000 bonus. As Hubspot says, some employees take vacations with their families, while others learn how to play the guitar, or enjoy month-long honeymoons.

Metis Communication in addition to three weeks vacation time lets its employees take their birthdays off and a bonus vacation week during the last week of December.

Metis Communication offers a four-week vacation after every five years of employment with them.

Metis Communication offers a Friday vacation day in the summer for all employees who have completed five years with the company.

Netflix recognizes that one of the most special events in an individual's life is the birth or adoption of a child. Our parental leave policy is: "take care of your baby and yourself." New parents generally take 4 - 8 months.

Learn more

Netflix has a vacation policy which states just two words - take vacation. Time away works differently at Netflix. They don’t have a prescribed 9-to-5 workday, so they don’t have prescribed time off policies for salaried employees, either. They don’t set a holiday and vacation schedule, so the employees can observe what’s important to them.

Learn more

SevenRooms a hospitality app requires employees to take five consecutive days off so they get a well-defined break.

Learn more

SevenRooms offers upto two weeks paid leave before new employees start to ensure the new employee is refreshed and relaxed in the new job.

Learn more

Deloitte offers incredible perks focused on parents and parents-to-be. Eligible employees, both moms and dads, can get up to 16 weeks of paid parental leave to bond with new babies after birth or adoption. The leave can also be used to care for a spouse, domestic partner, child, sibling or parent with a serious health condition. Eligible employees can even be reimbursed for up to $25,000 of expenses related to adoption or surrogacy.
