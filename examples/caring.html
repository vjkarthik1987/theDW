Supporting the employee's social goals

Salesforce follows the 1% Philanthropy Model where it pledges to dedicate 1% time, 1% product, 1% profit/ equity back into the community. Their Volunteer Time Off (VTO) program empowers employees at Salesforce to give back with their talent and time without using their Paid Time Off (PTO). Employees get to decide when, where, and which causes they want to support; whether it is volunteering virtually at home or onsite related to COVID-19 relief, natural disasters, racial equality and justice, and many more... the possibilities and opportunities are limitless. In total, each Salesforce employee are eligible to take up to 7 days (56 hours) of paid VTO each year.
