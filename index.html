About TDW

"Work is not where you go, but what you do"

The TDW aka The Dream Workplace is an open initiative by a group of friends working across various organizations who came together to create a collection of ideas that they believe can help organizations across the world implment so that they take care of the evolving needs of their employees. This is a result of our aspirations as well as our frustrations. We believe that many organizations across the world do not treat employees with the respect they deserve and practice policies and rituals that are decades old. We want to break it through our fictional employee handbook and 100s of examples. Our vision is to provide organizations and employees alike with insights which can be implemented at various organizations across the world in order to make the employees across the world happy with their orgnizations.
